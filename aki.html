Aki

Aki Hayakawa was a Public Safety Devil Hunter, working under Makima's special squad.[1] He had contracts with the Curse Devil and Future Devil, and a former contract with the Fox Devil. He was killed and possessed by the Gun Devil, thus becoming a Gun Fiend
